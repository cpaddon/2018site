Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any limits on number of appointments booked?

None. We're invested in you; we want your business to be successful, and we don't charge more just because you grow your business.

Can I change my plan?

Absolutely. You can change up to bronze, silver or gold at anytime with no penalty. You can also switch from monthly or annual billing at any time with pro-rated charges (and discounts on annual)

Is there an annual plan?

Definitely. You can select annual billing in the business dashboard; Annual plans get a 2 month discount off of a year!.

Do you offer a free trial?

We offer risk free accounts, meaning if you're not 100% happy with OnSched any time during the first 30 days; it's full money back.

Do you have customer support?

Rest assured that help is just a few clicks away. The little orange bubble in the bottom right of the screen will put you right in touch with support. We're here 24/7

Can I integrate with 3rd party applications?

We have 250+ pre-built integrations connect with through Zapier
