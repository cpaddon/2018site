OnSched Integration

Our integration with OnSched was done with two core concepts in mind: Brand Consistency and Automation. We created a booking flow that fit our design concept, then called OnSched's API in order to pull availability from all of our vendors, straight out of their Google and Outlook Calendars.

When a demo is booked from TechnologyAdvisors.com an appointment is created in OnSched's admin portal, then automatically sent to the vendors Google or Outlook Calendars along with a text-message and email notification.
